BROWN UNIVERSITY DINING SERVICES MARKETING PLAN

In collaboration with the Brown University Office of Sustainability, the Brown University Dining Services (from here on refered to as BUDS) requested different marketing materials and templates upon which they could expand their visual identity and reach.

All materials presented below were made by me.

Where Waste Goes Print Signs

Placed by the waste receptacles in the main dining hall at Brown, these waste signs are meant to educated the Brown community where their different forms of waste are transferred to after they leave the campus.

Local Food Map

To highlight the local food vendors through which BUDS purchases some of its food, BUDS requested a buildable local food map that can be updated with the variety of partner vendors. The Sharpe Refectory (The Ratty) Map

The main dining hall at Brown, the Sharpe Refectory, hosts a plethora of different stations catering to the different diets of the student body. BUDS requested a map showcasing the location of each to help incoming students navigate the terrain.
