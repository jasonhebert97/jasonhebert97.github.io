POSTERS & OTHER PRINT FORMS

All pieces displayed on this page were made with the intent of being printed off and placed around the campuses of either Dartmouth College or Brown University for advertising different topics and events.

Available Transportation Poster

A poster that details the different transportation methods available and their impact on the environment measured by carbon dioxide emissions.

Made using Adobe Illustrator

RIPTA Infographic

A one-pager highlighting the ways Brown community members can use the RIPTA -- Rhode Island's public transportation bus.

Made using Adobe Illustrator

Nitrogen Footprint Scientific Poster

A scientific poster depicting Brown University's current numerics regarding nitrogen footprint. This poster was presented at the 6th Annual Nitrogen Footprint Network Workshop at the University of New Hampshire.

Made using Adobe Illustrator

Waste Sorting Flowchart

A waste sorting flowchart used to guide Rhode Island based community members on the appropriate way to sort different materials using a yes/no system.

Made using Adobe Illustrator

Word of the Year

A poster made for Brown University's Office of Sustainability to bring attention to National Geographic's declaration of the Word of the Year.

Made using Adobe Illustrator

Martin Luther King Jr. Keynote Performance 2019

A poster made for Dartmouth College's 2019 Martin Luther King Jr. Celebration Keynote Performance announcing the speaker, Franchesca Ramsey. Made in coordination with and for Dartmouth College's Office of Institutional Diversity & Equity.

Made using Adobe Photoshop
