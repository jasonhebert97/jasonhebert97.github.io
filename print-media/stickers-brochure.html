Sustainability Brochure

The 2018 edition of the Sustainability Brochure released by Brown University's Office of Sustainability which introduces readers to the most useful sustainability resources around campus: transportation, waste sorting, and involvement.

Created using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. 3D rendering made using Adobe Dimension.

Sustainability Sticker

A sticker designed by and for Brown University's Office of Sustainability to highlight the five core sustainability elements deemed important by the office's interns and staff: transportation, compost, waste and solar, social justice, and the Urban Environmental Lab center.

Made using Adobe Illustrator and printed for dissemination around the community. 3D rendering made using Adobe Dimension.
