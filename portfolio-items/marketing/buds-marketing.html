Brown University Dining Services Marketing Plan

In collaboration with the Brown University Office of Sustainability, the Brown University Dining Services (from here on referred to as BUDS) requested different marketing materials and templates upon which they could expand their visual identity and reach. Working with two other interns, several marketing materials and timelines were created to aid in BUDS marketing expansion.

Printed Materials

Three specific posters were requested by BUDS for this marketing transition: a regional map depicting the local vendors BUDS partners with, an internal dining hall map locating the different sections in the main campus eatery, and a set of posters that describe the locations where all of Brown's waste goes.

All posters were made by me using a variety of Adobe Creative Suite applications.

Local Source Marketing Timeline

With contribution from the other interns, a local food sourcing marketing timeline was roughly developed for BUDS to adhere to throughout the academic year. This timeline details and organizes the different marketing pathways available to BUDS as well as suggesting how often these materials should be disseminated or updated.
